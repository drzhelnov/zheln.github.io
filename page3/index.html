I check PubMed for updates in the Systematic Review Subset, tag them with AMA specialties, and critically appraise the most appealing reviews daily on YouTube. My appraisals are very thorough, see Methods (DOI 10.31222/osf.io/y2nrb). This is independent research supported by crowdfunding only.
